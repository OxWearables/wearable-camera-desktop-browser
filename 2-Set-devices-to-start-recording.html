SET DEVICES TO START RECORDING

<do every time before giving devices to a new research participant>

1. CLEAR IMAGES FROM CAMERA (Using DohertyBrowser software)

<here we "download" images from the camera which automatically wipes the camera clean, thus making it ready to be used again>

    1. Navigate to your desktop, and click “DohertyBrowser”
    1. Click the dropdown box in the middle of the opening screen to select a user
    2. a. If you select “<new participant>”, then enter a ParticipantID in the new text box that appears
    1. Click the “START” button that appears
    1. Plug the camera into your computer via the USB cable
    1. Click the “Add” button
    1. Click the “Start” button (indicates software has identified plugged in camera)
    1. When finished, do not plug the camera out, unto the software allows you to click the application “Quit” button
    1. The images will now be deleted from the camera.

2. ACCURATELY SET CAMERA TIME (Using Autographer software)

<here we set the time of the camera to be the same as our PC>

    1. If connected, please disconnect the Autographer camera
    1. Open the “Autographer” software
    1. Select the “Settings” button on the left hand menu
      To get the most accurate synchronisation do the following as quickly as possible:
    1. Plug the Autographer camera into your computer via the USB cable
    2. a. If your computer does not acknowledge the device is plugged in, try a different port
    1. Press the synchronise clock button (under heading “Autographer”)
    1. Disconnect the Autographer camera from your computer

<The camera is now ready for a participant to use it>
