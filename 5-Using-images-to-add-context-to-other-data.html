IMPORT AND RELATE EXTERNAL DATA TO WEARABLE CAMERA IMAGES (Using DohertyBrowser software)

    1. Prepare your heartRate/GPS/accelerometer episodes into a CSV file the same format as:
    2. a. Navigate to https://sensecambrowser.codeplex.com/
    3. b. Click “Downloads” along the top menu bar
    4. c. Download “episodeDefsEg.csv”
    1. Open the software
    1. Create a new user, e.g. “P001Accelerometer”
    1. Navigate into the main screen
    1. Click the “Add” button
    1. Click “Advanced”
    1. Click “Download from PC” if downloading images from PC (generally this will be the case)
    1. Click “Browse” beside “Path of images already on your PC:”
    1. Navigate to the folder where the images are located
    1. Click “Browse” beside “Episode definitions:”
    1. Navigate to the .csv file you prepared in step 1
    1. Then click “Start”
